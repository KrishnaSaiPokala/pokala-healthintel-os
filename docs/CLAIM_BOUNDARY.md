# Pokala HealthIntel OS Claim Boundary

Pokala HealthIntel OS is a public-data healthcare intelligence and portfolio demonstration system. It is designed to evaluate market, reimbursement, safety-signal, provider-density, innovation, and strategy questions using public or synthetic artifacts.

## Allowed claims

- Browser-native healthcare intelligence SaaS prototype.
- Public-data strategy and evidence-lineage workflow.
- No-PHI architecture demonstration.
- Market, safety-signal, reimbursement, and innovation screening.
- Temporal modeling architecture and model-governance surface.
- Cloudflare Worker + static artifact deployment pattern.

## Disallowed claims

- Not a medical device.
- Not clinical decision support.
- Not diagnostic software.
- Not treatment guidance.
- Not HIPAA certification.
- Not production EHR integration.
- Not patient safety validation.
- Not proof of real-world clinical outcome improvement.

## Required caveat language

All scores are screening signals generated from public or demonstration artifacts. They require source review, domain review, and validation before any business, clinical, regulatory, or operational decision.
